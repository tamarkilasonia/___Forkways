The Problem We Solve

Many students, especially in tech and STEM fields, struggle to understand how their university subjects relate to real job opportunities.

Academic Confusion

Students feel lost in their academic journey without clear career connections to their studies.

Employment Gap

There's a disconnect between university education and industry needs, leading to low employability.

Motivation Drop

Without seeing the bigger picture, students become unmotivated and may even consider dropping out.

About _forkWays

We're revolutionizing how students connect their education to real-world careers.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic learning and professional employment by providing personalized career guidance and direct connections to opportunities.

Why _forkWays?

We solve a real problem of career misalignment, take advantage of digital education trends, and align with both university and industry needs.

Market Opportunity

First-mover advantage in Georgia and the Caucasus region with no localized competitors. The EdTech market is growing rapidly with increasing demand for digital career guidance.
